About Moo Deng

Moo Deng is a pygmy hippopotamus who became an internet sensation due to her adorable appearance and charming personality.

She was born on July 10, 2024 to parents Tony and Jonah at Khao Kheow Open Zoo in Si Racha, Chonburi, Thailand where she currently lives.

The name Moo Deng was chosen through a public poll, with over 20,000 people voting for it. It translates to 'bouncy pork' or 'bouncy pig' in English.

Some facts about Pygmy hippopotamuses

  1. Pygmy hippopotamuses are smaller than their larger relatives, the common hippopotamus. They typically weigh between 160 to 270 kg.
  2. They are native to the forests and swamps of West Africa, particularly in countries like Liberia, Sierra Leone, and Ivory Coast.
  3. Pygmy hippos are more solitary than common hippos and are primarily nocturnal, spending most of their time in dense vegetation near water sources.
  4. They have a unique adaptation that allows them to close their nostrils and ears when submerged in water, helping them stay underwater for extended periods.
  5. Pygmy hippos are classified as endangered due to habitat loss and hunting, with an estimated population of fewer than 3,000 individuals in the wild.
